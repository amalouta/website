PAINTINGS:

PAINTINGS is a series of process-based works executed in acrylic paint, inspired by and translated from dynamic geologic activity. This work incorporates my love of geology and its vitality with my attention to aesthetic, color, and obsessive, time involved projects. Each piece incorporated a unique inventive way of working with acrylic paint, mimicking natural as well a culinary processes such as pouring, molding, blowing, cracking, and peeling.

This artwork constitutes my honors senior thesis in Studio Art at Wesleyan University, and was displayed at the Zilkha Gallery in April 2012.
